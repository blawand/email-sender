Privacy Policy for SendSuite

Last updated: April 01, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes Our policies and procedures on the collection, use and disclosure of Your information when You use the SendSuite application (the "Service") accessible via the Website, and tells You about Your privacy rights and how the law protects You. This policy specifically addresses how we handle Google user data obtained through Google OAuth and the Gmail API.

We use Your Personal data, including data accessed via Google APIs, solely to provide and improve the Service. By using the Service, You agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy and the Google API Services User Data Policy.

Compliance with Google Policies: SendSuite's use and transfer of information received from Google APIs will adhere to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

Interpretation and Definitions

Interpretation

The words of which the initial letter is capitalized have meanings defined under the following conditions. The following definitions shall have the same meaning regardless of whether they appear in singular or in plural.

Definitions

For the purposes of this Privacy Policy:

  • Account means a unique account created for You to access our Service, authenticated via Google OAuth.

  • Company (referred to as either "the Company", "We", "Us" or "Our" in this Agreement) refers to SendSuite, operated by Bassel Lawand.

  • Google User Data refers to data accessed from Your Google Account via Google APIs, specifically including permission to send emails via the Gmail API (scope: `https://www.googleapis.com/auth/gmail.send`) and basic profile information for authentication (your email address).

  • Personal Data is any information that relates to an identified or identifiable individual. This includes Google User Data and any other information You provide, such as email content or recipient lists.

  • Service refers to the SendSuite application functionality, which allows users to send personalized emails using their connected Gmail account, accessible via the Website.

  • Website refers to the web interface for SendSuite, accessible from https://sendsuite.onrender.com/ (or the specific URL where the app is hosted, linked from the portfolio).

  • You means the individual accessing or using the Service.

Collecting and Using Your Personal Data

Types of Data Collected

Personal Data

While using Our Service, We access and use the following Personal Data:

  • Email address: Collected via Google OAuth for account identification and communication purposes.

  • Gmail API Send Permission: We request permission (OAuth scope `https://www.googleapis.com/auth/gmail.send`) to send emails *on Your behalf* through Your connected Gmail account. We do not request access to read, modify, or delete your existing emails.

  • Email Content and Recipient Data: Information You provide within the Service interface, such as the subject line, body content of emails you compose, recipient email addresses (entered manually or uploaded via CSV), and any attachments you upload for sending.

  • Usage Data: Collected automatically when using the Service (see below).

Usage Data

Usage Data is collected automatically when using the Service.

Usage Data may include information such as Your Device's Internet Protocol address (e.g. IP address), browser type, browser version, the pages of our Service that You visit, the time and date of Your visit, the time spent on those pages, unique device identifiers and other diagnostic data for the purpose of improving service functionality and stability. Usage Data is anonymized where possible and is not linked to the content of emails sent.

Use of Your Personal Data

The Company uses Personal Data for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain our Service: Primarily, to use the granted Gmail API permission to send emails composed and initiated by You through the Service interface. This includes processing recipient lists and attachments You provide.

  • To manage Your Account: To identify You as a user and manage your access to the Service via Google OAuth authentication.

  • To contact You: To contact You by email regarding Your account, service updates, security notifications, or support requests.

  • To monitor and improve the Service: Using Usage Data to analyze trends, monitor service performance, diagnose technical issues, and improve the user experience.

  • To enforce our terms and policies and comply with legal obligations.

Limited Use of Google User Data: Notwithstanding anything else in this Privacy Policy, the Google User Data accessed by the Service (specifically the permission to send emails via the Gmail API) is used *solely* to provide the core email sending functionality initiated by You within the SendSuite application. We do not use this access to read your emails, send emails not initiated by you through the app interface, or for any other purpose not directly related to the Service's explicit functionality. This data is not used for advertising purposes.

Storage of Personal Data

Account Information (Email Address): Your email address, obtained via Google OAuth, is stored securely to manage your session and identify you as a user.

Email Content, Recipients, Attachments: Data you provide for sending emails (subject, body, recipients from manual input or CSV, attachments) is processed temporarily by the Service solely to facilitate the sending operation via the Gmail API. This data is typically not stored persistently on Our servers after the send operation is completed or fails, except potentially in temporary logs for debugging or error analysis, which are securely managed and regularly purged. We do not build profiles based on this content.

Google OAuth Tokens: Secure tokens required to maintain your connection to Google and send emails are stored securely using industry-standard practices. These tokens are specific to the permissions you grant (sending email) and do not grant Us broader access to your account.

Sharing Your Personal Data

We do not sell Your Personal Data, including Google User Data.

We may share Your Personal Data in the following limited situations:

  • With Google: To interact with the Gmail API for sending emails as You direct, based on the permissions You granted.
  • With Service Providers: We may use third-party Service Providers for hosting, infrastructure, and monitoring purposes (e.g., Render for hosting). These providers process data on Our behalf under strict confidentiality agreements and do not have permission to use Your Personal Data, especially Google User Data, for their own purposes. They are only permitted to access data necessary to perform their specific functions for Us.
  • For Legal Requirements: We may disclose Your Personal Data if required to do so by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities (e.g., a court order or government agency), but only to the extent required and legally permissible.
  • For Security and Abuse Prevention: We may share information if necessary to investigate, prevent, or take action regarding potential policy violations, suspected fraud, situations involving potential threats to the safety of any person, or as evidence in litigation.
  • Business Transfers: If the Company is involved in a merger, acquisition or asset sale, Your Personal Data may be transferred. We will provide notice before Your Personal Data is transferred and becomes subject to a different Privacy Policy. Google User Data transfers will comply with Google's policies.
  • With Your Consent: We may disclose Your Personal Data for any other purpose with Your explicit consent.

We will *not* share Your Google User Data with affiliates, business partners, advertisers, data brokers, or other third parties except under the specific, limited circumstances described above (primarily for service provision, security, legal compliance, or with your consent).

Retention of Your Personal Data

The Company will retain Your Personal Data only for as long as is necessary for the purposes set out in this Privacy Policy. We will retain and use Your Personal Data (like your email address for account management) to the extent necessary to provide the service, comply with our legal obligations (for example, if we are required to retain your data to comply with applicable laws), resolve disputes, and enforce our legal agreements and policies. Temporary data like email content for sending is retained only as long as needed for the operation and associated logging (see Storage section).

Usage Data is generally retained for a shorter period of time for analysis purposes, except when this data is needed to strengthen the security, improve the functionality of Our Service, or We are legally obligated to retain this data for longer time periods.

Transfer of Your Personal Data

Your information, including Personal Data, is processed at the Company's operating offices (conceptually) and potentially on servers provided by hosting Service Providers (e.g., Render) located in various jurisdictions. This means that this information may be transferred to — and maintained on — computers located outside of Your state, province, country or other governmental jurisdiction where the data protection laws may differ than those from Your jurisdiction.

Your consent to this Privacy Policy followed by Your submission of such information represents Your agreement to that transfer.

The Company will take all steps reasonably necessary to ensure that Your data is treated securely and in accordance with this Privacy Policy (including compliance with Google's policies for Google User Data) and no transfer of Your Personal Data will take place to an organization or a country unless there are adequate controls in place including the security of Your data and other personal information.

Delete Your Personal Data

You have the right to delete or request that We assist in deleting the Personal Data that We have collected about You.

You can revoke SendSuite's access to your Google Account at any time via your Google Account security settings page (https://myaccount.google.com/permissions). Revoking access will prevent the Service from sending further emails and effectively deletes the access tokens We hold.

You may request the deletion of your stored account information (your email address) by contacting Us using the details below. Upon request, we will delete this information, provided we do not have a legal obligation or overriding legitimate basis to retain it.

Please note, however, that We may need to retain certain information (like logs related to legal compliance or security investigations) when we have a legal obligation or lawful basis to do so, even after access revocation or account deletion request.

Security of Your Personal Data

The security of Your Personal Data is important to Us. We use Google OAuth 2.0 for authentication, meaning We never see or store Your Google password. We implement administrative, technical, and physical security measures designed to protect your data from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, and destruction. This includes using secure connections (HTTPS) for data transmission and secure storage practices for sensitive information like OAuth tokens.

However, remember that no method of transmission over the Internet, or method of electronic storage is 100% secure. While We strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect Your Personal Data, We cannot guarantee its absolute security.

Children's Privacy

Our Service is not intended for anyone under the age of 13 (or the applicable age of digital consent in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from anyone under this age. If You are a parent or guardian and You are aware that Your child has provided Us with Personal Data or connected their Google account, please contact Us. If We become aware that We have collected Personal Data from anyone under the relevant age without verification of parental consent, We take steps to remove that information and terminate the account access.

Our Service may contain links to other websites that are not operated by Us (e.g., links within this policy to Google). If You click on a third party link, You will be directed to that third party's site. We strongly advise You to review the Privacy Policy of every site You visit.

We have no control over and assume no responsibility for the content, privacy policies or practices of any third party sites or services.

Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update Our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify You of any significant changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date. We may also inform you via email or through a notice within the Service prior to the change becoming effective.

You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes. Changes to this Privacy Policy are effective when they are posted on this page.
